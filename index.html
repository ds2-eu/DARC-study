DataSpace DataShare 2.0

Interaction and Experience of Intelligent Assistance in Inter-organizational Data Exchange

Thank you for your interest in participating in our study, "Interaction and experience of intelligent assistance in inter-organizational data exchange". This study explores your experience interacting with the DARC intelligent conversational agent developed within the EU DataSpace DataShare 2.0 (DS2) project. Your feedback will help improve trustworthy data sharing solutions and guide the development of the DARC chatbot.

Participation Steps

Please attempt the following three tasks during your interaction with the DARC chatbot. Use the chatbot as you naturally would. Describe what you are trying to do, ask questions, and follow up if something is unclear. There is no required script or sequence of steps.

  1. Get to know the EU DS2 project and its software modules.
  2. Explore available data assets: What data assets are relevant for your domain or interests? What kind of details about those data assets would you like to know? Explore the data assets and metadata available to you.

    Tip: The catalogue of data assets used in this study is for testing purposes and is therefore limited. If you do not find data relevant to your domain and interests, consider exploring the following example: Imagine you are interested in air quality trends. Use the DARC chatbot to discover relevant data, details about that data, and learn how you can gain access to it.

  3. Explore organizational data sharing: What data assets do you or your organization have ownership of that you would like to share with other individuals or organizations? Are there specific conditions under which you would like to share that data? Explore how DS2 modules can support you to publish and share your data.

    Tip: If you do not have a personal data sharing use case, consider using the following example: Imagine that you own a dataset on passenger flow in a city's public transportation system. You wish to make this data available to researchers and city planners in other data spaces. Use the DARC chatbot to find out how the DS2 modules can support you to publish and share your data, and what you would need to put in place around access control and data rights.
